About

Ready.

My name is Marissa and I'm a student at the University of Colorado-Boulder. I'm a senior studying information science with a minor in business and a certificate in TAM (technology, arts & media)- and I've loved every second of it! Along with academics, I love sea kayaking in the summer and excel in petting dogs and hammocking. I am an avid outdoors lover and am interested in the intersection between humans, technology, and the outdoors.

I am originally from Denver, Colorado, but moved to Boulder, Colorado to attend the University of Colorado Boulder. I am beyond grateful for the life I live. I have had numerous opportunities to travel and find new hobbies. I have a passion for the outdoors and spend my summers teaching teens how to sea kayak up in the San Juan Islands, Washington. I grew up playing piano, violin, singing, and dancing and continue to keep music and rhythm in my life as much as possible! 

I'm also an avid dancer, and am currently on three teams at my school: Boulder Bole Bhangra, CU Milana, and VeRVE Collisionz. I am the captain and founder of CU Milana, the Bollywood fusion dance team. This team is not only the first of its kind at CU Boulder, but also the first in the state of Colorado and also the first within the mountain range!! I'm very proud of the work this team has accomplished in its first year! I am also the current co-captain of Boulder Bole Bhangra, CU's bhangra-hip hop team. This team is in it's 6th year and only goes up! The final dance team is the school's street dance team. Here, I am the human resources head and help to mediate any conflict between team members and also team members to leadership.
