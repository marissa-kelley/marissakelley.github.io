About

Hello!

My name is Marissa and if I were to be any animal, I would consider myself something like a duck. I don't have wonderfully orange feet that make funny sounds on hardwood floors or on drums, although I am a huge fan of the color orange, but because I like to live like a duck. Michael Caine's quote, "Be like a duck. Calm on the surface, but paddling like the dickens underneath" exemplifies the way I like to work, learn, and enjoy the things I do- calm and collected, but full of zest!

I grew up in Denver, Colorado and moved to Boulder to attend the University of Colorado Boulder where I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in information science through the College of Media, Communication & Information (CMCI). Additionally, I received my minor in global business from the Leeds School of Business and my certificateÂ in technology, arts & media (TAM) from the Atlas Institute. I've loved every second of it- especially the people (and dogs) I've met along the way! To see some of the projects I've done, head over to the "Projects" tab. To learn about the work and research I've done, head to the "Profile" tab.

Outside of classes, I keep busy through dance, which has become my favorite creative outlet and I am always willing to learn a new style! During the summers I work as a sea kayak instructor at a YMCA camp in Washington, where I enjoy the change in outdoor scenery and hang out with some really cool teens. Check out my "Fun" page for more details.
